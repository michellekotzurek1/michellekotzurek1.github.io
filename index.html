ABOUT ME

I'm Michelle - a UX Designer with a degree in social work. Coming into UX with a social work background and mindset helps me to explore deeper how environments, sociology and psychology affect how people use and relate to products.

You can currently find me in Malmö but being raised in three different countries all over the globe I call many places home. When I'm not elbows deep in design projects you can catch me pursuing my three passions in life: asian food, gardening and fantasy litterature.
